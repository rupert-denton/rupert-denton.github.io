A description of your strengths and limitations concerning learning.
I’m generally a self-starter, I have high-expectations of myself, want to get a lot from this course and do an outstanding job so will be organised to do so. What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Managing the workload

Keep it respectful, provide space for others to speak before me, be sincere and constructive in engaging with everyone, receive feedback graciously and provide it respectfully.

When things build up, take a step back, write the things down so they aren’t in my head but on paper - I generally find this makes mental mountains feel much smaller and more achievable.
